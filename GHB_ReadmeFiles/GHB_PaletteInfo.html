Palette documentation

You probably be wondering, where does the CCC bits in YXPCCCTT point to what color in the palette editor? Well, the CCC refers to what arrays of color on the palette editor, numbered from 0-7 inclusive. On a 2BPP (2 bits per pixel) format (which the status bar graphic uses), each tile possess 4 colors, and each color occupies 2 bytes, stored in binary as 0bbbbbgggggrrrrr (r g and b means the red, green and blue colors, each ranging from $00-$1F inclusive).
